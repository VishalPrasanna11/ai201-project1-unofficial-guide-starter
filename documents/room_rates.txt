UNDERGRADUATE FIRST YEAR HOUSING RATES 2025-2026

Important Notes:
- Standard and Economy Rates in traditional first-year halls are designated based on room size. For more room information and building accommodations, visit First Year University Housing webpage.
- The majority of first-year rooms are double, triple, or quad occupancy. Students can designate interest in single rooms on their housing application and we will fill vacancies in enrollment deposit date order.
- Meal Plan Required: First Year students living in meal plan required buildings (do not have kitchens) require at least the 180 Meal Plan. First Year students living in apartment style buildings are required to have at least the 150 Meal Plan.
- All First Year Housing is Meal Plan Required
- All buildings and price points are subject to change. University reserves right to alter these designations at any time. During certain terms, First Year students could be assigned to any university residence hall, even those without First Year designation. All rates are charged semesterly.
- ^ Designated spaces reserved for students with classified & documented Housing Accommodations only.

UNDERGRADUATE UPPERCLASSMEN HOUSING RATES 2025-2026

Important Notes:
- Meal Plan required: Continuing students (second year and above) living in meal plan required buildings require at least the 100 Meal Plan. First Year students living in meal plan required buildings (do not have kitchens) require at least the 180 Meal Plan. First Year students living in apartment style buildings are required to have at least the 150 Meal Plan.
- Traditional Style Halls: Bedrooms with shared floor bathrooms. Meal plan required.
- Suite-Style Halls: Bedrooms with bathroom. Some meal plan required.
- Apartment Style Halls: Bedrooms with kitchen/kitchenette and bathroom.
- Studio Apartments: apartments where living/kitchen area and bedroom are the same room.
- All buildings and price points are subject to change
- ^ Designated spaces reserved for students with classified & documented Housing Accommodations only.
