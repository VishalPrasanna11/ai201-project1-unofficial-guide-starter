Northeastern University Residential Utilities

Basic Utilities
All Northeastern University housing, including leased properties, covers basic utilities as part of Room and Board:
- Water
- Electric
- Heat

Additional utilities provided by Northeastern detailed below.

Cable and Internet

Internet Services by Housing Type

University Owned Housing:
- Internet: NUwave
- Cable: Xfinity On Campus™ Streaming

Leased Property Apartments:
- Internet: Self-selected with university credit
- Cable: Xfinity On Campus™ Streaming

60 Belvidere:
- Internet: NUwave
- Cable: Xfinity On Campus™ Streaming

Internet Details

NUwave Internet
Available within university-owned properties and 60 Belvidere.

Leased Properties Internet
Leased property apartments do not have NUwave. Internet can be set up through outside provider. Students receive $100 credit per semester ($50 per summer term) on student account in sixth week of term (third week of summer term) to assist with internet costs.

Cable Access

Xfinity On Campus Streaming
All students living in university housing have access to Xfinity On Campus streaming services. Residence halls do not have active coaxial cable connections.

Service Details:
- Provided through partnership with Comcast
- Delivered through Xfinity On Campus™ portal
- Offers online access to 200 broadcast and premium channels
- Includes on-demand content
- Available to students living in any University Housing

Access Requirements:
- Must be connected to Northeastern network (ResNet or NUwave) to access all content
- Limited content available from remote locations
- Must be enrolled at Northeastern
- Access begins on first day of classes for incoming students
- Must be living in University Housing on Boston Campus

Laundry

Laundry in University-Owned Buildings
All university-owned properties have designated laundry facilities. No payment required - simply load clothes, select cycle, and press start.

Customer Service Contact:

- Email: Service@automaticlaundry.com
- Web: automaticlaundry.com

Resources available for:

Laundry in Leased Property Apartments
In-building laundry varies by building, accessible to residents at their own cost. Residents granted access to nearby residence hall to utilize residential laundry rooms following university-owned buildings guidance. Specific locations assigned to building by semester, communicated to students via Northeastern email by hall staff. Laundry access for LP apartment students active 6am-11pm daily.

Laundry in 60 Belvidere
60 Belvidere residents have access to designated laundry room within building. Students issued $75 credit each semester in Housing Husky Dollars to cover cost of 1 load of laundry (wash and dry) per week.

Housing Husky Dollars Details:
- Applied to resident accounts at beginning of each semester
- Can be used like Husky Dollars
- Expire annually in August
- Not refundable/transferrable
- Credits intended to offset laundry cost but cannot be used for direct laundry payment

Use machines via My Magic Pass App (directions posted within laundry room).

Printing

Annual Printing Credit
Members of Northeastern community receive $120 printing credit on Husky Cards at start of academic school year. Can be used at various color and black & white printers across campus.

Credit Details:
- Non-refundable
- Annual credit does not roll over year to year
- Once $120 spent, printing system draws from Husky Dollars

Printing Information:
- All jobs sent to printing queue available to print for up to 12 hours
- Cost determined on per-page basis
- Varies depending on paper size and printer type used

Students can track:
- Print balance and transactions
- Number of print jobs submitted
- Number of pages printed

Access via Papercut Printing Portal.

Mail

Mail Code System
All university housing residents, including those in Leased Properties, issued unique mail codes for campus mail addressing. Students keep 6-digit Mail ID entire time at Northeastern. All packages and paper mail delivered and distributed through university mailrooms and package locker network. No mail delivered directly to residential halls.

Sample Address Format:
Student Name
6-Digit Mail ID
716 Columbus Ave.
Boston, MA 02120

Mail Information Access
New students receive mailing information about two weeks prior to start of each term. Subsequently available to reference on Housing Online Portal. Mailing addresses available on student's Housing Online portal on that timeline, remain available throughout time at Northeastern.

Package Acceptance Timing
No packages accepted for any students for term until 2 weeks prior to that semester's move-in. Not recommended to send essential items to campus via university mail prior to move-in, as packages can be delayed at start of term. For alternative ship-to-room services before Fall, additional resources available on vendor information page.

Prohibited Items

Perishable Items
Perishable items such as food or flowers cannot be accepted by Residential Mail. This includes any form of COD (call on delivery) packages and subscription deliveries from services including:
- Amazon Prime Market
- Edible Arrangements
- HelloFresh
- Insomnia Cookies
- Blue Apron
- Freshly
- Home Chef
- Omaha Steaks
- Daily Harvest
- Georgetown Cupcakes
- Imperfect Foods

If absolutely necessary for perishable package to be delivered (i.e., for medical purposes), call or email residentialmail@northeastern.edu before items are sent so arrangements can be made.

Alcohol Policy
Packages containing alcohol will be returned to sender. Alcohol not permitted to be accepted at university mailrooms.
