Northeastern University Housing Application & Selection

Application Process

Students receive all email communication to their Northeastern email account. Contact Housing Office with questions.

Incoming First Year Students

Housing Requirements
Undergraduates entering as first-year students in Boston through fall admission and NUin must live in university housing during their first and second years, unless academic or co-op experience takes them outside Boston area.

Beginning Fall 2026: First-year students entering Boston or Oakland through fall admission and NUin program are required to live in university housing for their first two years, whether in Oakland or Boston for second year, unless academic or co-op experience takes them outside those cities.

Living Learning Communities (LLCs)
First-year students entering through fall admission live in Living Learning Communities - themed housing environments connecting students of similar academic and personal interests. LLCs enrich academic experience outside classroom and help form lasting bonds with students sharing same values and activities.

Application Process for First Year Housing
1. Pay enrollment deposit (includes housing deposit) and claim Northeastern Student Account
2. Access Housing Application on Application Status Check under Housing Online
3. Allow 24-48 hours from deposit payment for Housing Online access
4. Complete application by May 7, 2026 (can be edited until that date)
5. Enrollment deposit must be paid by May 1, 2026

Required students who do not complete housing application or are approved to live off campus through Residency Waiver Process will be assigned randomly.

Assignments for first-year students processed in enrollment deposit date order.

Move-In Timing
Students must arrive during scheduled move-in date and time. If unable to arrive during assigned time, must select alternate arrival date and time from dynamic list of available time slots for their building. Arrival dates and times are limited per date, time, and building. Access Move-in Time Slot Change process in Housing Online.

Important Dates
- Fall Entry 2026: Application due May 7, 2026; Assignments summer 2026 in enrollment deposit date order
- Spring Entry (NUin) 2027: Application November 2026; Assignments mid-to-late December 2026 in enrollment deposit date order

Transfer Students

Housing for transfer students (incoming and continuing) is not guaranteed or required.

Incoming Transfer Students
University housing for transfer students and students in third year and beyond is limited and on space-available basis. These students live in mix of university and off-campus housing in Boston or participate in co-op, study abroad, or learning opportunities outside Boston.

Currently, university housing not available for transfer students being admitted for upcoming academic year. No transfer student waitlist exists. New transfer students informed of housing availability at time of admission.
