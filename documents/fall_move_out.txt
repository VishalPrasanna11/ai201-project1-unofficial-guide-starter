Northeastern University Fall Move-In/Out

Fall Move Out

Move Out Deadline
Students must move out by 24 hours after their final exam or by 7pm on December 13. Students must follow hall staff's move-out procedures and check out appropriately. Failure to check out properly may result in lost/discarded property, fines and/or judicial action.

Move Out Checklist
- Remove all belongings and dispose of trash, recycling, and perishables into building's designated areas
- Close and lock windows
- Leave room in good condition to avoid damage or excessive cleaning charges
- Ensure bed and furniture are free of all belongings for cleaning
- Complete the Self Check-Out form in Housing Online when fully vacated
- Indicate any existing damage on Room or Apartment Condition Form during Self Check-Out process
- Return key or key card to hall staff if applicable

Residents will be billed for damages beyond normal wear and tear.

Moving Hampers
Students can check out a moving hamper with government-issued photo ID (driver's license or passport). One hamper per student. Return hamper as soon as possible to allow access for other students.

Hamper Availability Schedule:
Speare Hall: December 5-16 (various hours from 8AM-8PM)
Davenport A: December 12-14 (8AM-8PM)
West Village G: December 12-14 (8AM-8PM)
Limited hampers at 60B: December 11-14
Limited hampers at RSO: December 17-January 2

After-hours returns: Bring hamper to Residential Safety Office in upper lobby of Speare Hall to retrieve photo ID.

Parking Passes
Date-specific parking and unloading passes available for Columbus Garage and North Lot. Print correct pass for move-in date and location.

Available dates:
- Columbus Garage: December 5-15, December 26
- North Lot: December 6-7, December 12-14, December 26

No parking available for oversized vehicles (over 6'8" including roof racks) or overnight parking. All complimentary parking ends no later than 11pm. West Village E Garage closes and locks at 11pm. Contact housing@northeastern.edu for ADA parking support.

Fall Move In

Timing and Dates
Fall move-in takes place over 7-9 days around Labor Day Weekend leading up to first day of classes. Specific dates and times sent to students over summer based on room assignment. Students should not book travel until receiving official move-in date and time.

Summer II housing students receive Move-to-From date for mid-August to move directly from Summer II space to Fall space.

Move support only available for certain buildings on specific days. Early arrivals not always available and move-in services may not be available on alternate dates.

Preparing for Move-In
Students assigned specific move-in date and time via Northeastern email. Date change requests sometimes available.

Recommendations:
- Bring essentials and few personal touches
- Purchase additional items near campus or have items shipped after settling in
- Keep belongings manageable for potential summer term room transitions
- Review What to Bring page for recommended items list
- Vendor services available: summer storage, microfridge rentals, blue bike services

Parking: No oversized (over 6'8" including roof racks/storage) or overnight parking provided.

Details for Fall Move In 2026 including dates by building, parking, maps, and driving directions will be posted in August.
